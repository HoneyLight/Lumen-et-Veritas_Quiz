Lumen Quiz Competition

The Lumen et Veritas Academy quiz competition is a yearly competition that place in the month of June. This competition has been of great assistance to many beneficiaries as it provides the winner with scholarships and International research programs and tours.

The start date for this year's quiz competition is on June 1st, 2023.

Please note that the deadline for registration is on the 31st of May, 2023. All interested schools and student representatives are expected to register on or before 29th May, 2023.
